Introduction

This is a team event. Each team will contain 3-5 members. Each team will be provided a specific area of our college to survey and thereafter make a map on chart paper according to their survey. After that the teams have to make a 3-D model according to their map and submit their models to their respective coordinators. At last, the best model will be declared as the winner.

Flow of Event


Registration
The registration form will contain Team details which will include- Team members names, branch, email-id, roll number, WhatsApp number.
Team Leader name, branch, email-id, roll number, WhatsApp number.

Demonstration
A brief description about the event will be given to all the teams for their better understanding of the event, along with clearing their doubts.

Area Allocation
A specific area will be allotted to each team for surveying and preparing a map (On a chart paper).

Map Submission:
Each team has to submit its map on the scheduled time.

Map Correction:
In case there are any corrections in the map, they will be corrected.

Model Making:
The teams have to prepare a 3-D model of their respective allotted areas based on their map prepared in the previous stage.

Assembling:
The models of all the teams will be assembled together to make a complete 3-D model of BIET, Jhansi.


Result declaration/Prize Distribution:
The team which prepares the best model will be announced as the winner and will be awarded.


Who can Participate?


Only 1st and 2nd year students from BIET Jhansi can participate in this event. No external participation is allowed for this event.

Rules and Regulations

  • Only the students from first and second year can participate in this event.
  • Each team can contain a maximum of 5 members.
  • The scale of the map for each team should be as follows: 1 cm2 = 0.032 m2
  • Each team is responsible for the security of its model (until submission).
  • Use of any type of electronic gadget is prohibited.
  • The materials required for the model will be provided by the college.
  • Discipline should be maintained throughout the event and no mischievous or offensive activity will not be tolerated.

Time alloted for various events:

2 days for surveying the allotted area. 1 day for preparing 3-D model. 1 day for assembling all the models and to prepare a complete model of BIET Jhansi.
