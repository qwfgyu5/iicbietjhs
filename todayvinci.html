Introduction

“The science of today is the technology of tomorrow.”

Inspired from these words Innovation and Incubation Cell presents “Todays Vinci”, Science Exhibition. In this event participants have to design and present innovative models portraying the theme SOLUTION FOR MODERN DAY PROBLEMS. Leonardo Da Vinci was a painter, engineer, and scientist known for his drawing and notes on variety of subjects, Da Vinci had an enviable understanding of engineering principles and implementing them in practical life.


Theme of Event

“If I had an hour to solve a problem, I would spend 55 minutes thinking about solution.”

Participants have to make innovative models or projects which demonstrate SOLUTION FOR MODERN DAY PROBLEMS. Example: Global Warming, Pollution, Traffic, etc. Participants can also make any other working model, prototypes, drones, etc. Participants who use Da Vinci’s ideas and models will be awarded extra points.
For reference to Leonardo da Vinci’s ideas visit:
https://www.leonardo-da-vinci.net/inventions/

Flow of Event


Registration:
Participants need to register through the registration form which will be released. The teams will need to fill their team leader and other member details. They will also need to submit their model idea in brief.

Short Listing:
The teams will be shortlisted on the basis of projects description submitted. The teams shortlisted during this phase will be invited to present their model during the exhibition.

Exhibition:
The shortlisted teams will have to display their models and explain their projects using charts and other documents. The exhibition will be conducted and space will be allocated in Incubation Centre Seminar Hall.

Evaluation:
Models will be evaluated by jury members which will be from academia and industry. Marking Scheme:
- Creativity and Imagination – 20%
- Originality and Scientific Innovation – 20%
- Technical Skills and Workmanship – 10%
- Utility to Society and Scalability – 20%
- Economic Viability (Low Cost) – 20%
- Presentation – 20%
- Using Vinci’s idea and model – 10%

Award Distribution:
Based on jury and evaluations, results will be declared. Assured certificates for all participants. Lucrative prizes for winners.


RULES AND REGULATIONS

1.Each team can have 4 to 5 members.
2. Teams will have to keep the deadlines in mind. Extension beyond deadline will lead to disqualification.
3. All participants should maintain discipline and follow the instructions by the organizing team.
4. Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
5. Participants must be studying in class 9th or higher.
6. All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
7. All teams from school should be accompanied by teacher assigned by the school.
