Introduction

There are times when you have ideas in mind, but you are unable to present them. At the pitching event, your stage fear will be tested. Your sheer will to present your ideas on time and resources will be tested.

Flow of Event


Registration:
Registration for the event will start 6-7 days prior to the main event.

Demonstration:
Registered candidates will be briefed about the event.

Abstract Submission:
After the briefing, the teams will have to submit their idea in brief (abstract), which will be evaluated and top 15 teams will be shortlisted.

Presentation:
Each team will be given 3 minutes to present (pitch) their idea in front of the jury. The presentation will be followed by questions from the jury. The teamâ€™s performance will be judged accordingly.

Result Declaration/Prize Distribution:
The team with the best idea and presentation skill will be awarded. Assured certificates for all participants.

Who can Participate?


This event is open to all. Any team from any recognized institution or college can participate.

Rules and Regulations


1. Participants need to register by filling out the registration form. Participants can team up to 3 members and details about each member must be clearly stated in the registration form.
2. After the registration, participants will be asked to submit their ideas. The reference template for the Pitch presentation is attached below.
3. The shortlisted participants will be informed about the date and time of their final pitch.
4. The shortlisted participants will pitch their ideas to an esteemed panel of jury members.

Note about the pitch presentation:
1. The presentation is required strictly to be made on PowerPoint or google slides.
2. It should not contain any illegitimate elements.
3. Teams are required to be formally dressed during the presentation.
4. Participants may use any language for presentation, but try to avoid switching of languages.
